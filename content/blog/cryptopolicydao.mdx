  <strong>Lobbying for Web3-friendly Regulation</strong>

Crypto Policy DAO is a platform for anyone to collaborate together to provide clear insight into establishing web3-friendly regulation.
Our goal is to provide clear and concise insight into what and why certain aspects of the blockchain require web3 legislation and how to create an initial
draft for it.
